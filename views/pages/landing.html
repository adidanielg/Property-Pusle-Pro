FAQ

Questions? We have answers.


Do I need a credit card to start? +
No. Your 14-day Pro trial is completely free — no credit card required. You only need to add a payment method when your trial ends and you choose to subscribe.
Can I change my plan later? +
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time. If you upgrade, the change takes effect immediately. If you downgrade, the change takes effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
What happens when I hit my plan limits? +
You'll see a notification inside the app when you're approaching your limit (80% usage). When you reach the limit, you'll be prompted to upgrade — your existing data is never deleted.
Is PropertyPulse available on mobile? +
Yes. PropertyPulse is a Progressive Web App (PWA) — open it in Safari or Chrome, tap "Add to Home Screen," and it installs like a native app on iOS and Android. No App Store required.
Can my technicians use it too? +
Absolutely. Technicians have their own portal where they see assigned jobs, update ticket status, and receive push notifications — all from their phone.
Is my data secure? +
Yes. PropertyPulse uses Supabase (PostgreSQL) with encrypted connections. All authentication uses JWT tokens in HttpOnly cookies, and role-based access ensures data is always isolated between accounts.
