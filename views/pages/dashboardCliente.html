Step 1 of 5

Add your first property

Properties are the foundation of PropertyPulse. Add the addresses you manage so you can attach maintenance tickets to each one.

💡 Tip: Your Starter plan includes 3 properties. You can always upgrade later for more.

🎫
Step 2 of 5

Create your first ticket

Tickets are maintenance requests. Each one is tied to a property and can be assigned to a technician. Add photos, descriptions, and priority levels.

💡 Tip: Be specific with the description — it helps your technician diagnose the issue faster.

🔧
Step 3 of 5

Invite a technician

Share the technician registration link with your maintenance team. They'll create their own account and start receiving job assignments.

Technician registration link

💡 Tip: Your Starter plan includes 1 technician. Upgrade to Pro to add up to 5.

👤
Step 4 of 5

Complete your profile

Add your phone number and company name so your technicians know who they're working with. You can update this anytime from the Profile section.

💡 Tip: Scroll down to the Profile section at the bottom of your dashboard to edit your details.

📱
Step 5 of 5

Install the app

Add PropertyPulse to your home screen for instant access — no App Store required.

📱 iPhone (Safari): Tap the Share button → "Add to Home Screen"
🤖 Android (Chrome): Tap the menu (⋮) → "Add to Home Screen"
